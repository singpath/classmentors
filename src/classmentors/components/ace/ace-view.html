The IMDA 2017 National Coding Championships National Infocomm Competition will take place on April 30th, 2017.

There will be a junior and senior category. The password to join all events is "junior" or "senior" unless a teacher from your school has requested to change the password.

  1. Please login to ClassMentors.com by clicking the login button above.
  2. If you are new to ClassMentors, you will need to enter a unique public ID after you login (example: chris123).
  3. After you are logged-in and have completed registering, click on the junior or senior cohort below and then click on the event for your school.
  4. From the school event page, click the Join button, enter the password ida, and press enter.
  5. Once you have joined an event, go register for an account at CodeCombat.com and complete the first level. This should only take you about 5 minutes.
  6. Then come back to ClassMentors and update your profile with your CodeCombat username. This will allow ClassMentors to see how many CodeCombat achievements you have completed.

If you see a school missing in the rankings below, please encoruage them to participate. If your school is not listed, you can join the All Other Junior or All Other Senior events.

You can visit the Ace of Coders Facebook page to leave comments, ask questions, and join in the conversation.

Thank you!
